Navia Education

Brand Positioning

Navia is a future-focused education and consulting company serving individuals, education institutions, organizations, and corporate clients.

We empower individuals to advance their education, careers and life choices. We invest into education institutions, support organizations in managing human capital transitions, and help corporations optimize their people strategies through skills development, coaching, and strategic workforce planning.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to bridge the gap between potential and opportunity by delivering personalized education and career coaching and tailored upskilling programs that prepare individuals and teams to thrive in the age of AI and constant disruption.

Vision Statement

Our vision is a world where the workforce is informed, adaptable, and empowered — where every individual and organization unlocks the full potential of human capital in a rapidly evolving, AI-driven landscape.

About Us

Everyone deserves a fulfilling education experience and career, and the tools to shape their future. At Navia, we help individuals navigate through life-changing education and career decisions and empower them to take bold, informed steps toward their next offer — whether that’s a new job, new school, new skill, or new purpose.

We also help organizations and employers build agile, future-ready teams by designing people strategies that align with business goals and talent aspirations.

Navia is where education meets evolution — and where potential becomes possibility.

Meet the Founder

Dr. Shang Gao is an education, technology, and skills development expert with nearly two decades of experience spanning academia, international development, and corporate leadership.

Born and raised in a small city in China, Shang grew up with a deep appreciation for education as a path to opportunity. Her early experiences shaped her passion for unlocking human potential — especially in environments where resources and access could be a limitation. A lifelong learner and driven purposefully by equity, Shang believes that with the right support, anyone can design and pursue a meaningful, fulfilling career.

Shang holds a PhD in Economics of Education and a Master’s in International Public Policy. Her career has taken her from the halls of Columbia University, where she interned at the United Nations Headquarters in New York, to impactful roles at the World Bank Group and Asian Development Bank, where she served as an Education Economist driving national policy reforms and workforce strategies across Asia and Sub-Sahara Africa.

Most recently, Shang served as a Senior Executive at Amazon Web Services (AWS), where she led digital transformation strategies for governments, universities, and public sector organizations across the Asia Pacific and European region.

Through Navia Education, Shang brings her unique blend of experience in public policy, international development, corporate leadership, and technology to empower both individuals and organizations. Her mission is to help others navigate the complexities of modern careers — with clarity, courage, and confidence.

She now lives in Singapore with her husband and two young children — her daily inspiration and reason to build a more inclusive, future-ready world.
